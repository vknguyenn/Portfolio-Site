About Me!

Before venturing into software engineering, I pursued a degree in Health Science, graduating with honors. This academic journey, though distinct from technology, instilled in me a foundation of discipline and the value of hard work. The realization that my passion lay in technology, rather than health science, came as I was close to the completion of my degree. Encouraged by my close friends and family, I decided to shift my career path towards software development, a field where my interest had always resided.

My experience at App Academy has been transformative, sharpening my technical skills in state and database management, and programming fundamentals, while also enhancing my problem-solving abilities. The development of my capstone project, Fumblr, allowed me to integrate technical skills with creative design, culminating in a user-friendly web application. This project, along with my journey through App Academy, highlighted my adaptability and my capacity for continuous learning. My journey from health science to software engineering, marked by resilience, adaptability, and a unique blend of technical and non-technical strengths, makes me look forward to being a part of the tech industry.
